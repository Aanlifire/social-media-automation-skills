# Source Policy

Use this policy to classify evidence for travel short-video topic research.

## P0 Official Sources

Use for core facts and time-sensitive claims.

- official tourism bureau, culture/tourism department, government announcement
- airport, railway, metro, bus, airline, hotel, attraction, museum, park, event organizer
- official platform announcements or API documentation
- official weather, transport, ticketing, or public-safety notices

Examples of supported claims:

- opening/closure notices
- holiday crowd-control announcements
- ticket or reservation rules
- official event dates
- transport changes

## P1 Authoritative Sources

Use for trend evidence and market context.

- reputable news organizations
- established travel and business media
- OTA or travel-platform trend reports
- industry research reports
- large local media with named reporting

Examples of supported claims:

- destination popularity
- booking trend direction
- holiday travel behavior
- broad budget or hotel-flight trend signals

## P2 Community and Creator Sources

Use for pain-point discovery, not as sole evidence for hard facts.

- high-quality travel blogs
- forums and Q&A
- creator posts with clear firsthand context
- public comments or complaints
- GitHub/data projects if relevant

Examples of supported insights:

- route feels tiring
- common first-time mistakes
- family/couple/student concerns
- visual ideas and content language patterns

## P3 Search Snippets and Open Web Fragments

Use only as discovery hints.

- search result snippets
- low-context aggregation pages
- unattributed listicles
- SEO farms

Do not use P3 as the only source for factual claims.

## Conflict Handling

When sources conflict:

1. Prefer P0.
2. Prefer newer sources for time-sensitive claims.
3. Prefer named, attributable reporting over anonymous summaries.
4. If still uncertain, mark the topic as requiring fact check.

## Risk Flags

Flag the topic if it depends on:

- current prices
- opening hours
- visa or entry rules
- safety incidents
- health claims
- user-generated accusations
- copyrighted footage availability
- platform policy-sensitive automation

# Source Policy

## P0 Official

Use for hard facts:

- tourism bureau
- attraction official site
- official ticketing page
- metro/rail/airport/bus operator
- government notice
- official event organizer
- official weather or safety notice

## P1 Authoritative

Use for trend and market context:

- reputable news outlets
- established travel platforms
- OTA reports
- named local media reports
- industry research

## P2 Community

Use for pain points, not hard facts:

- travel blogs
- forums
- creator posts
- public comments
- Q&A

## P3 Weak Signals

Use only for discovery:

- search snippets
- anonymous aggregators
- SEO pages
- unsourced summaries

## Claim Handling

- Hard current facts need P0 or strong P1 with date context.
- Trends need at least one P1 or multiple consistent P2 signals.
- Subjective claims should be rewritten as opinions or removed.
- Unsupported claims should not proceed to publishing.

# Brand Policy

## Positioning

Position the AI travel-planning agent as a practical assistant for:

- route order
- budget planning
- day-by-day itinerary planning
- avoiding detours
- comparing options
- turning comments into route ideas

## Soft-Ad Standard

The content should still work if the viewer ignores the brand.

Good insertions:

- solve a visible problem
- appear after the problem is established
- connect directly to the travel planning pain
- use concrete input/output language

Poor insertions:

- praise the product without context
- repeat the brand too often
- make unverifiable savings promises
- interrupt the story too early

## Placement Guidance

- Hook: no brand unless the format is explicitly "AI planned my trip".
- Midroll: one natural line.
- Endcard: one clear CTA.
- Caption: short CTA plus comment prompt.
- Watermark: brand name only or very short phrase.
